An implementation of deep neural network models for image denoising and blind inpainting, based on Xie, Xu, and Chen's "Image Denoising and Inpainting with Deep Neural Networks" (NeurIPS 2012). Separate pipelines train and evaluate denoising and inpainting models on natural and medical images, using datasets such as Flickr1024, DIV2K, and BSDS300.
