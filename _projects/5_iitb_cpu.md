A 16-bit RISC processor featuring a 6-stage pipeline architecture, designed as part of the Microprocessors course at IIT Bombay. Implements hazard mitigation via forwarding, flushing, stalling, and branch prediction, with individual components — instruction memory, pipeline registers, controller boxes, register file, ALU, MUXes, and sign extenders — simulated in VHDL on Intel Quartus.
